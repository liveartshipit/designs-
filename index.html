Different Types of Design

Styles

  • Minimalist: Clean, simple, lots of whitespace.
  • Typography-Based: Focus on font and text layout.
  • Illustrative: Custom illustrations and artwork.
  • Photographic: Uses photos as main element.
  • Vintage/Retro: Nostalgic, classic design cues.
  • Abstract: Shapes, colors, and textures.
  • Geometric: Patterns, lines, and symmetry.
  • Hand-drawn/Sketchy: Artistic, casual feel.
  • Gothic/Edgy: Dark, bold, dramatic.
  • Modern/Futuristic: Sleek, tech-inspired.

Applications

  • T-shirt Design: Fit, fabric, purpose-driven.
  • Mug Design: Wraparound graphics, handle placement.
  • Bottle Design: Ergonomics, viewing angles.
